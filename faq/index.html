How should I start?
Install application, complete registration, choose the brand you would like to represent. After that GoLogo official will contact you and schedule appointment for decal installation, after decal is installed you just open application and press “Start”.
How does GoLogo install decal?
Driver arrives to meeting location, the car will be washed and decal will be installed. Decal installation will take 30 mins, and both decal and car wash will be absolutely free.
How GoLogo counts mileage?
We use smartphone GPS module to count the mileage only. We also can’t track your current location and don't gather the history of it.
What are points?
Points are used to measure in the application. GoLogo point equal to 1 kilometre multiplied by coefficient. And to get the coefficient, GoLogo uses a unique system where all of the calculations are based on zone, vehicle class, time and day. Drivers will get more points during rush hour on the second ring than at 1AM on the fifth ring road, for example.
What is point Verification?
At the end of the campaign, the driver will have verify points earned during the campaign, there are two options for this process. The first option is to set up an appointment with a GoLogo agent to check the mileage in person. A GoLogo agent will remove the advertisement decal, and clean the driver’s car. The second option is for drivers who cannot meet in person with a GoLogo agent. These drivers can take pictures of their meters (real time only) and send it to GoLogo. Decal removal for this choice is left to the driver. After points are verified they will be automatically moved from current campaign to drivers profile.
How long should the decal be placed on the vehicle?
Driver should keep the decal on the car till the last day of campaign and it can be removed by a GoLogo agent or the driver will remove the decals after point for current campaign are verified.
Will decal damage the car surface?
No. We are using the best materials available on market for our decals. Decal installation and deinstallation will be provided by GoLogo officials, we can guarantee no damage to car surface.
Can I have more than one decal on my car at the same time?
No, you only can take part in one campaign at a time.
Right after I finished the first Campaign, can take part in another one?
Yes, after first campaign decal is deinstalled by GoLogo agent, you can join next campaign and new decal will be installed immediately at the same location.
What vehicles can take part in Campaign?
It should be your personal car, we will ask you to provide car registration documents during decal installation. Also, car surface shouldn't have damages.
