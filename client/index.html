CPM

GoLogo calculate the impression count by overlaying the GPS data collected daily from every car, on top of traffic algorithms.
Important variables that our model takes into consideration include the

- average daily traffic count on the roads
- the number of lanes
- the average load per vehicle
- and the time of day

The traffic volume is weighted in consideration with the amount of time the GoLogo spent on that specific road. Impression counts are collected daily.
